The Briefing Centre is at your service

We inspire global policy makers, political leaders, corporate executives, investors, and international media with the success story of the Research for Georgia Team, guide them on the paths and build links to leading external consulting providers.

Hub in Braga

At the Research for Georgia Briefing Centre, we present visitors with our concept produced exclusively for the Georgian Centre for Strategy and Development , guiding them along the paths of External Governance & Security while playing an integral role in promoting the country’s know-how and expertise, share the lessons learned and challenges, the nation status and the future of it, and act as coordinators between G2G, B2G, and B2B relations.

We also host global political leaders such as presidents, ministers, high-level decision-makers, private sector representatives, investors, and international media keen to learn more about Georgia. We can assist and consult other countries with goverment evaluation initiatives and match them with credible solutions to empower their efforts, as well as boost innovation and international cooperation. We travel globally, delivering keynotes, moderating panels, and actively engaging in discussions to share invaluable insights at international events.

Established in 2024, we are today part of a University of Minho iniciative.
